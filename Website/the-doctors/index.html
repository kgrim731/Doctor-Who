The Doctors Homepage

The Doctor is the last of the Time Lords. The sole survivor of Gallifrey and The Last Great Time War. He has travelled space and time, battling evil for over 900 years. On the run from his past in an amazing ship. He hates burnt toast. He loves a fez. And if you’re in his way, you better run.
